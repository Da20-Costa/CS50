My passion: Soccer!

As a proud Brazilian, I can say that soccer runs in my blood. One of my greatest passions is watching matches, especially when my team, Flamengo, is playing. Based in Rio de Janeiro, Flamengo is not just a club; it's a phenomenon with one of the largest fanbases in the world, boasting over 40 million supporters. What I love most is the style of play we cherish in Brazil: a beautiful, creative game filled with skillful dribbling and pure joy. We call it "futebol-arte" (the art of football), a philosophy that values flair over rigid tactics. I'm incredibly proud of my country's legacy in the sport. Brazil is the only nation to be crowned Pentacampeão—five-time FIFA World Cup champions—and our history is filled not with mere players, but with true artists of the beautiful game, like Pelé, Garrincha, Zico (a Flamengo legend!), and Ronaldinho.
